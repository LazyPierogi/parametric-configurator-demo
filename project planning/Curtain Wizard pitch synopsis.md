# Curtain Wizard pitch synopsis

### **Slide 1 – Opening Statement (Purpose)**

*"We make home design simple and beautiful — starting with curtains."*

A bold new way to transform living spaces with **AI-powered simplicity**.

---

### **Slide 2 – The Problem**

- Buying curtains today is **complex, slow, and frustrating**.
- Manual measurements, endless showroom visits, unclear pricing.
- Customers struggle to **visualize the final look** before buying.
- Result: wasted time, poor fit, dissatisfaction.

---

### **Slide 3 – The Solution**

- **AI-powered configurator**: measure any room from a single photo.
- Choose fabrics & styles with smart recommendations.
- **Instant realistic preview** of curtains on the user’s own photo.
- Easy drag-and-drop adjustments for rods and placements.
- Instant price, instant order.
- Add-ons: stylist consultations, pro installation, rods & accessories.

---

### **Slide 4 – Target Audience**

- **Homeowners & renters** (aged 30–50) in major Polish cities.
- Middle to upper-middle income, design-conscious, tech-savvy.
- Value **quality, aesthetics, and convenience** over lowest price.
- Lifestyle: invested in creating a **beautiful, functional home** for themselves and their family.

---

### **Slide 5 – How It Works (Technology)**

1. User takes a **photo of their room**.
2. AI engine auto-detects window dimensions & context.
3. User selects fabric, color, or uses AI recommendations.
4. **Live visualization**: curtains instantly appear in user’s photo.
5. Automated order processing connects with curtain producers & installers.
6. Scalable model: direct sales + **B2B licensing** of configurator abroad.

---

### **Slide 6 – Purpose, Vision, Mission**

- **Purpose**: Redefine how people shop for home décor.
- **Vision**: Become the go-to platform for personalized, AI-driven interior solutions across Europe.
- **Mission**: Save time, reduce friction, and deliver a **professional, beautiful result** with just a few clicks.
